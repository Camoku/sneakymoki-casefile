CAM-02DESIGN CALLS● REC

        
The calls that made it tick

        
One choice, whole game. Sneak = slow & silent. Run = fast & LOUD. That single trade-off is the entire tension — and it never stops mattering.

        
The chase is scary on purpose. Spotted doesn't mean instant-fail — the rat runs you down, and you have to break line of sight to lose it.

        
Traps scare, rats catch. Cameras & lasers only sound the alarm and send the pack to you. Clean, readable rule.

        
  • Fear package
  • heartbeat + red pulse
  • pack-join squeals
  • relentless speed-up
  • gentle reset

      


      

        
CAM-03THE TOOLBOX● REC

        
Gadgets on the belt

        
A kit of toys that all read at a glance — and thanks to a modular build, adding one new gadget instantly works in every level ever made.

        
  • 🍞 throw-to-lure snacks
  • hide-in trashcans
  • keys + matching gates
  • reversing lockpick dial
  • teleport vents
  • pressure plates

      


      

        
CAM-04TEACH / NO WORDS● REC

        
It teaches without a single word

        
The masterstroke: the title screen plays the game at you — a ghost Moki sneaks, throws snacks, runs, rescues & hides on loop. Players learn just by watching the menu.

        
Backed by camera sweeps that whisper “look around,” glowing hidden Tamakins that chirp to bait your curiosity, and a “held overhead” pose the first time you grab a snack.

      


      

        
CAM-05VISUAL IDENTITY● REC

        
The whole thing is a security feed

        
You gave it one cohesive look: a hacked surveillance monitor. Scanlines, glitches, a REC dot, live timecode, a boot-up sequence, cycling CAM POVs, even a procedural humming room-tone.

        
  • CRT screen curve
  • glow / bloom
  • glitch shader
  • FEED PAUSED
  • 🔴 live REC

      


      

        
CAM-06ENGINE ROOM● REC

        
The tech flex under the hood

        
Levels are data, not hand-built scenes — so they can be mass-produced. You built a drag-and-drop level editor with a procedural room generator and a live “is this even beatable?” solver.

        
  • 25-level campaign
  • ascending difficulty
  • modular piece library
  • 🌐 live on the web
